About Us

Who We Are

At Buland Auto-Tech, we are passionate about road safety and smart transportation solutions. With years of experience in civil construction and modern vehicle services, we have expanded into providing High Security Registration Plates (HSR plate) across Delhi NCR and beyond.

Our mission is simple: make roads safer, smarter, and more reliable for every commuter, family, and business.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every vehicle is secure, identifiable, and connected—making traffic enforcement, accident prevention, and urban mobility efficient and reliable. Through HSR plate and digital road safety initiatives, we aim to bridge the gap between traditional vehicle management and the future of smart transportation.

Our Story

Buland Auto-Tech started as a civil construction firm, building roads, bridges, and infrastructure projects across Delhi NCR. Over time, we recognized a critical need for modern road safety solutions, especially HSR plates, which are essential for secure and traceable vehicles.

Today, we combine our experience in construction and urban planning with cutting-edge digital and security technologies to deliver a trusted HSR plate service. Every plate we provide represents quality, compliance, and safety, contributing to smarter roads and safer communities.
