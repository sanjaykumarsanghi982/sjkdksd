Privacy Policy | Official HSR Plate

At Official HSR Plate, we value your privacy and are committed to ensuring that your personal information is handled with the highest level of security and confidentiality. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you book your HSR Plate online through our website.

Information We Collect

To process your HSR Plate application smoothly, we collect the following details:

  • Full Name
  • Vehicle Registration Number
  • Contact Number
  • Email Address
  • Payment Information (via secure gateway)
  • Chassis & Engine Number (as applicable)

We only request information that is essential to complete the booking and registration process.

How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used solely for the purpose of:

  • HSR Plate registration and appointment scheduling
  • Sending booking confirmations and service updates
  • Responding to your support queries and feedback
  • Ensuring secure and verified online transactions
  • Improving user experience on ParivahanPortal.in

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties under any circumstances.

Data Security

We take your security seriously. Our website uses SSL encryption and secure payment gateways to protect your personal and payment data. All sensitive information is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel.

Use of Cookies

We may use cookies to:

  • Enhance your browsing experience
  • Track website performance and user behavior for analytics
  • Remember preferences for faster booking on return visits
